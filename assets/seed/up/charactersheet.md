## Carl Fredricksen

![Carl Fredricksen](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/524984ff-b5da-44fd-a9b3-9f420c23bf87/carl15.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Protagonist |
| **Age** | 78 |
| **Species** | Human |
| **Occupation** | Retired balloon salesman |
| **Signature item** | Grape soda cap pin |

Carl Fredricksen is not your average hero. He's a retired balloon salesman who, at the age of 78, is forced to leave the house he and his late wife Ellie built together. But instead of moving into the old folks' home, Carl takes action. He ties thousands of balloons to the roof, lifts the house into the air, and sets off toward South America, finally fulfilling the promise he made to his wife so many years before.

## Russell

![Russell](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/f60b5a51-414a-46a7-8194-b283da0b0bb4/russell12.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Deuteragonist |
| **Age** | 8 |
| **Species** | Human |
| **Occupation** | Junior Wilderness Explorer, Tribe 54, Sweat Lodge 12 |
| **Signature item** | Wilderness Explorer sash & enormous backpack |

Russell is an enthusiastic and persistent 8-year-old Junior Wilderness Explorer. Armed with a backpack laden with official camping gear, Russell is ready for the wilderness — there's just one catch: he's never actually left the city. All his knowledge of the outdoors comes from books, and his sole camping experience was in his own living room.

## Dug

![Dug](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/8e8487f3-4642-4b15-a46a-e62f38002632/dug9.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Supporting hero |
| **Age** | Adult |
| **Species** | Golden Retriever |
| **Occupation** | Pack dog / tracker (defected) |
| **Signature item** | High-tech thought-translating collar |

Dug is a lovable golden retriever living in the wilds of Paradise Falls as part of the dog pack searching for a rare flightless bird. Like the rest of his pack, Dug is outfitted with a remarkable high-tech collar that translates his thoughts into speech. But Dug is mocked as the nerd of the pack. As they are pursued through the jungle, the sweet but simple-minded Dug must decide which pack he actually belongs to.

> *"I have just met you, and I love you."*

## Kevin

![Kevin](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/0dbf6f34-d035-4a16-bd7d-0c5a8d4e8f2d/kevin13.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Supporting hero |
| **Gender** | Female (named Kevin by Russell) |
| **Species** | Rare flightless bird (scientifically unknown) |
| **Height** | 13 feet tall |
| **Habitat** | Paradise Falls, South America |
| **Signature trait** | Iridescent feathers; sweet tooth |

Named by Russell, this extremely rare, 13-foot-tall flightless bird is hidden from the world in her remote Paradise Falls habitat. With brilliant iridescent feathers and a long, flexible neck, Kevin is exceptionally fast and nimble despite her size. Russell names her Kevin after he discovers they share a sweet tooth. Kevin joins the jungle's latest and most unlikely pack: Carl, Russell, and Dug.

## Charles F. Muntz

![Charles F. Muntz](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/bd39bb0e-2cdb-49ac-93bc-21a4528baaa4/muntz10.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Antagonist |
| **Species** | Human |
| **Occupation** | Explorer, adventurer (fallen from grace) |
| **Vessel** | *Spirit of Adventure* — a half-mile-long art deco airship |
| **Signature phrase** | *"Adventure is out there!"* |

In the early 1930s, rich, clever, and handsome Charles F. Muntz is a beacon of hope for a down-and-out American public — and the idol of young Carl and Ellie. Traveling the globe in his massive self-designed airship, he discovers the world's treasures. But when scientists discredit the skeleton of a fantastic creature he brings back from South America, Muntz vows to return and capture it alive. He won't come back until he does — and decades of obsession have left him anything but the hero he once appeared to be.

## Alpha Pack

![Alpha Pack](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/d07f1d05-2b6d-4c4a-af74-033e4767aea6/alphapak12.jpg?format=1000w)

| Member | Breed | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Alpha** | Doberman Pinscher | Pack leader |
| **Beta** | Rottweiler | Alpha's lieutenant |
| **Gamma** | Bulldog | Hench-dog |

Deep in the jungles of Paradise Falls resides a dog pack on a mission to capture a rare bird for their master. Just like Dug, all pack dogs have high-tech collars that give them unusual abilities — including GPS tracking and thought-to-speech translation. Alpha is a menacing-looking, black-as-night Doberman with authority no one dares question. Nothing will detract the pack from its mission — except maybe a squirrel.

## Ellie

![Ellie](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/60241cb68df65b530cd84d95/b1c13043-42f8-4f89-9577-d49cff59b948/elle9.jpg?format=1000w)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Carl's late wife; emotional core of the story |
| **Species** | Human |
| **Occupation** | Zookeeper |
| **Signature item** | The Adventure Book |
| **Dream** | Travel to Paradise Falls, South America |

Ellie is an exuberant, adventurous girl who dreams of exploring the world. She and Carl first bond over their shared admiration for legendary explorer Charles F. Muntz. They pledge to travel together to Paradise Falls — the world's tallest waterfall, deep in the South American rainforest. As the couple settle into their married life, their grandiose plans are set aside as they navigate the ups and downs of everyday life. Ellie's spirit and memory are the emotional engine that drives Carl across an entire continent.

> *"Thanks for the adventure — now go have a new one!"*
